Why Digital Wellness Matters

With smartphones, social media, and remote work, we spend more time in front of screens than ever before. Excessive device usage can lead to eye strain, sleep problems, anxiety, and decreased attention span.

Common Symptoms of Digital Overload

  • Eye fatigue and dryness
  • Neck and shoulder pain
  • Decreased sleep quality
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Increased social anxiety

Practical Wellness Tips

üí° The 20-20-20 Rule

Every 20 minutes of screen use, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds. This reduces eye strain effectively.

üåô Pre-Sleep Digital Detox

Stop using devices at least 1 hour before bedtime. Blue light suppresses melatonin and affects sleep quality.
